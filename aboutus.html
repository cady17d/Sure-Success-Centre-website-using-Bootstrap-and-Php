About Us

Our History

Sure Success Centre is the name that spell confidence, estabilised in 1996 , founded by Sri Sunil Kumar Jaiswal to provide best educational guidlines, preparation and training for SSC, Railway, Banking, IBPS, UPSC, JPSC, sector for students.

Sure Success Centre SSC

Faith of the youth- ‘Our determination’ Sure Success Centre (Circular Road, Ranchi), an extremely renowned educational institution of Jharkhand, is known for providing guidelines on the preparation for various competitive exams (written & interview) like (PO/Clerk), SSC, Railway, CDS, NDA, CPF, CPO etc. The institution, founded on 15th July 1996 by Sri Sunil Jaiswal adopted the policy of the best quality in the field of guidelines, studying minutely the pattern of various competitive exams, good management, best faculty and good execution of decipline etc. It has borne fruit and more than 25,750 candidates have finally been selected in various sectors across the country after getting the guidelines from the institution. The institution is famed not only in Jharkhand but also in Delhi, M.P. Chhatisgarh, Odissa, Bihar, U.P. & West Bangal. It always makes an effort to get the candidate Sure Succeeded. It provides regular classes, weekly classes, special test based on the pattern of various competitive exams, the weekly classes of current affairs (mainly on economy), up to date study materials, library facility etc. There is a cardial & co-operative relation between the teacher and the students here. It always tries to enhance the mental ability of the students which facilitates their success. Our motto is ‘the Success of the student’.
